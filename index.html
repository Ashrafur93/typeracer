Instructions

Welcome to TypeRacer! Follow these instructions to take the typing test:

  1. Select the difficulty level from the dropdown menu on the right.
  2. Read the sample text displayed in the left column.
  3. Start typing the sample text in the typing area below it.
  4. Your time and words per minute (WPM) will be calculated as you type.
  5. Once you finish typing, your results will be displayed below the typing area.
  6. If you want to try again, click the "Retry" button.

Good luck and have fun!
